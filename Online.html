How the online tutoring process works at Mathisi Learning?

  1. Scheduling and Communication:
    • Parents or students reach out to Mathisi Learning to schedule an online tutoring session via Discord.
    • Once the session is scheduled, tutors and students/parents exchange Discord usernames to connect.

  2. Connection and Setup:
    • At the agreed-upon time, both the tutor and the student join a private voice or video call on Discord.
    • Before the session begins, the tutor ensures that the necessary materials (e.g., textbooks, worksheets) are readily available.

  3. Assessment and Goal Setting:
    • The tutor begins the session by assessing the student's current understanding of the subject matter.
    • Based on this assessment, the tutor and student/parent discuss specific learning goals and areas of focus for the session.

  4. Instruction and Practice:
    • The tutor provides instruction on relevant concepts, using a combination of explanations, examples, and interactive exercises.
    • Throughout the session, the tutor encourages active participation from the student, answering questions and providing guidance as needed.

  5. Customized Learning Experience:
    • Depending on the student's needs and preferences, the tutor may tailor the session to focus on specific topics or areas of difficulty.
    • Parents may request a particular focus for the session, such as homework help, test preparation, or skill reinforcement.

  6. Transparency and Accountability:
    • All tutoring sessions are recorded to ensure transparency and accountability in the learning process.
    • The recorded sessions are accessible to both the tutor and the student/parent for review purposes, allowing for a comprehensive understanding of the session content and interactions.

  7. Feedback and Progress Tracking:
    • At the end of the session, the tutor provides feedback on the student's progress and performance.
    • The tutor may also suggest additional resources or strategies to support ongoing learning outside of tutoring sessions.

  8. Follow-Up and Future Sessions:
    • After the session, the tutor and student/parent discuss any follow-up actions or assignments for the student to complete before the next session.
    • If desired, future tutoring sessions can be scheduled directly through Mathisi Learning, maintaining the same online format via Discord.
