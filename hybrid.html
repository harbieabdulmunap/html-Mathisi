Overview of the hybrid tutoring process with Mathisi Learning, including transportation fees:

  1. Scheduling and Location:
    • Parents or students contact Mathisi Learning to schedule hybrid tutoring sessions, which combine both online and in-person elements.
    • Locations for in-person tutoring sessions are arranged based on convenience and availability, such as the student's home, a local library, or another suitable public space.

  2. Tutor Assignment:
    • Mathisi Learning assigns a qualified tutor who can accommodate both online and in-person tutoring sessions based on the student's needs and preferences.

  3. Initial Meeting:
    • The tutor and the student/parent meet in person for an initial consultation to discuss learning goals, expectations, and any specific areas of focus.
    • During this meeting, the tutor also explains how the hybrid tutoring model will work and addresses any questions or concerns.

  4. Session Preparation:
    • Before each tutoring session, the tutor prepares materials and resources based on the student's learning needs and the topics to be covered.
    • For online sessions, the tutor ensures that the necessary technology and resources are readily available.

  5. Instruction and Practice:
    • During the tutoring sessions, whether online or in-person, the tutor provides instruction, guidance, and practice exercises to help the student understand and master the subject matter.
    • The hybrid model allows for flexibility in the delivery of instruction, accommodating the student's preferences and learning style.

  6. Feedback and Progress Tracking:
    • The tutor provides feedback on the student's progress and performance during each session, as well as recommendations for further improvement.
    • Progress tracking may include assessments, quizzes, or regular check-ins to monitor the student's development over time.

  7. Follow-Up and Future Sessions:
    • After each session, the tutor and student/parent discuss any follow-up actions or assignments for the student to complete before the next session.
    • Future tutoring sessions, whether online, in-person, or hybrid, can be scheduled directly with Mathisi Learning for continued support and learning.

  8. Transportation Fee:
    • Please note that transportation fees for in-person tutoring sessions may vary depending on the distance traveled by the tutor to the tutoring location.
    • The transportation fee will be communicated upfront and may be included in the overall tutoring cost.

The hybrid tutoring model with Mathisi Learning offers the benefits of both online and in-person instruction, providing flexibility, personalized support, and access to qualified tutors. Please keep in mind the potential transportation fee variation for in-person sessions when scheduling hybrid tutoring sessions.
